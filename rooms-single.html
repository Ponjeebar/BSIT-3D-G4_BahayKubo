About this place

Experience authentic Filipino hospitality in this beautifully designed room at Bahay Kubo Resort. This spacious and well-appointed accommodation is ideal for up to 4 guests, featuring modern amenities, premium bedding, and stunning views of the resort's lush gardens and natural surroundings. Perfect for families, couples, or small groups seeking a peaceful and memorable getaway.

Enjoy the tranquil ambiance of our resort with easy access to our on-site restaurant, swimming pool, and recreational facilities. The room includes complimentary high-speed WiFi, air conditioning, and a 65-inch QLED TV with streaming services. Wake up to the sound of nature and experience the perfect blend of traditional Filipino charm and modern comfort.

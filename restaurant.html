Our Food

Experience the authentic flavors of the Philippines at Bahay Kubo Resort's restaurant. Our talented chefs prepare traditional Filipino dishes using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, bringing you a true taste of Filipino hospitality and culinary heritage.

From classic favorites like Chicken Adobo and Sinigang to our Chef's Special Platter and fresh seafood selections, every dish is crafted with care and passion. Whether you're craving comfort food or exploring new flavors, our diverse menu has something to satisfy every palate. Order now and let us bring the best of Filipino cuisine to your table!
